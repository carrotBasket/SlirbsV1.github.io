Slirbs

v1.0.0

Setup

Unfold the board. Split the green team from the brown team and equally split item cards. Shuffle brown cards with the items, and green cards with the other half of the items. Give the green team player the green deck and give the brown team player the brown deck. Put both decks faced down, and each player draws 3 cards (If there is not a Slirb card in your hand, reshuffle your hand into the deck and draw 3 other cards). Each player gets 11 pawns and places them around each base. The game is ready to begin.

Gameplay

The youngest player gets the first turn. They will move up to 3 pawns 1 square in any direction to capture a square, or add 1 pawn to the board (the square where the pawn is placed must be captured). If there is an enemy pawn in a 5-square radius, you may attack. Draw a Slirb card and take out a piece of paper to keep track of money and energy (the board will show how much you will get per attack). Choose an attack or move for you Slirb, minus energy and/or money on the paper, and apply the effects. It is time for your opponent to do the same. When a Slirb runs out of health, the loser discards the card(and the pawn), while the winner keeps theirs. The winner also captures all squares in the 5 square radius, besides the ones with an enemy pawn on it. Your turn is over after the battle and you draw a card from the deck. This goes on until one of your pawns goes onto the enemy base, in which you win.
